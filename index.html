Privacy Policy for Erana Banana Pinterest App

Effective date: April 2026

Erana Banana uses this internal Pinterest app to access and organize its own Pinterest business account content.

Information accessed

This app may access Pinterest account information, boards, board sections, pins, pin titles, pin descriptions, destination links, image metadata, and related Pinterest content data.

How the information is used

The information is used only to audit Pinterest boards, improve board organization, recommend Pinterest SEO improvements, and create reports for Erana Banana’s own Etsy-related marketing workflow.

Information sharing

Erana Banana does not sell, rent, or share data collected through this app.

Data storage

Pinterest data may be stored locally in project files such as JSON, CSV, and markdown reports for internal business analysis.

Data deletion

Stored local files can be deleted by Erana Banana at any time.
